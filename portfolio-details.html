Web application for sales and inventory management for non-perishable goods stores

  • Administrator and vendor user access
  • Insertion and listing of supplier data
  • Insertion and listing of equipment or machinery data
  • Insertion and listing of equipment or machinery purchase data
  • Insertion and listing of invoiced and non-invoiced sales
  • Insertion and listing of expenses
  • Insertion and listing of users
  • List of sales support over 5 years old
  • Automatic backup for sales over 5 years old
  • Purchase, sales and expense reports based on dates and displayed as a pdf document
